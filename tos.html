Code of Conduct 2026

1.0 Operative Identity

By entering the Ministry Grid, you agree to represent yourself under a singular operative handle. Impersonation of the Directorate or other established agents is a violation of the primary frequency.

2.0 Signal Transmission

All signals (messages) and assets (images) transmitted to the Archive are shared across the collective. The Ministry is not responsible for unsanctioned data leaks resulting from operative negligence.

3.0 Yellow Alerts & Warnings

The Directorate reserves the right to issue Signal Warnings for minor protocol breaches. Acknowledgment of these warnings is mandatory for continued Grid access.

4.0 Signal Termination

Major protocol violations will result in an immediate and permanent Sanction. Upon termination, your signal is excluded from the Grid, and your local repository is purged. Decisions made by the Directorate Terminal are final.

5.0 Grid Maintenance

The Ministry of Groove Grid is subject to periodic Lockdowns and Maintenance Wipes. The Directorate may purge public frequencies to optimize system performance without prior notice.
